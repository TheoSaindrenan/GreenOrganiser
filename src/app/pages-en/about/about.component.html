Green Organiser

What is Green Organiser?

Green Organiser is an independent agency specialising in the sale of hotel rooms for groups, exclusively in eco-friendly establishments.

We believe it is possible to travel with others without compromising your values. That is why every booking we offer is designed to limit environmental impact while ensuring comfort, simplicity, and efficiency.

Our mission:

Facilitating the organisation of group trips, while encouraging more responsible tourism.

In practical terms, this means:

1

Respond to all requests for 5 rooms or more

2

Select and negotiate with hotels committed to environmental sustainability

3

Acting with transparency and independence: no imposed hotels, no fixed offers

4

Offering a simple, fast and personalised service

Who is behind Green Organiser?

Green Organiser was founded by Mathis Gandubert, a passionate advocate of hospitality and ecology, with a degree in international hotel management, extensive experience in revenue analysis and operations in the luxury hotel industry, and now a committed entrepreneur.

After working in 4- and 5-star hotels, I came to a realisation:

Groups lack a simple, flexible booking service that is in tune with current climate issues.

This idea gave rise to Green Organiser.
