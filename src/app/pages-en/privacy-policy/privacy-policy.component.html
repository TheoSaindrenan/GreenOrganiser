Privacy Policy

Last updated: 02/10/25

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform users (hereinafter “Users” or “Customers”) of the website and form provided by GREEN ORGANISER (hereinafter “the Company”) about how their personal data is collected, processed and protected.

2. Data collected

When using the form and services, the Company may collect the following data:

  • Identification information: surname, first name, position, company
  • Contact details: e-mail address, telephone number
  • Information related to the planned stay: desired destination, dates, number of participants, budget, preferences
  • Browsing data (IP address, cookies, form usage statistics)

3. Purposes of processing

The data collected is used to:

  • Analyse and process customer quote requests
  • Connect customers with partner hotels
  • Monitor the commercial relationship and provide after-sales service
  • Improve the quality of service and offers
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Legal basis for processing

The processing of personal data is based on:

  • The performance of a contract or pre-contractual measures (processing of quote requests)
  • The explicit consent of the User (via the online form)
  • The legitimate interest of the Company (commercial follow-up, service improvement)

5. Recipients of the data

Personal data may be transmitted:

  • To partner hotels in the context of processing the request
  • To technical service providers (hosting providers, management tools) involved in the provision of the service
  • To administrative or judicial authorities, if required by law

6. Data retention

Personal data is retained for the period necessary to process the request and for the commercial relationship, then archived for a maximum period of 3 years from the last contact, unless otherwise required by law.

7. Users' rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the French Data Protection Act, Users have the following rights:

  • Right of access to their data
  • Right of rectification
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, Users may contact the Company at the following address: Contact@greenorganiser.com

8. Data security

The Company implements all necessary technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, unauthorised access, disclosure or alteration.

9. Cookies

The website and form may use cookies and tracking tools for statistical purposes and to improve the service. Users may configure their browser to refuse cookies, at the risk of limiting certain features.

10. Modification of the Policy

The Company reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. The version in force is the one published on the website or attached to the form on the date of use.

11. Applicable law

This Policy is governed by French law. Any dispute relating to its interpretation or execution shall be brought before the competent courts within the jurisdiction of the Company's registered office.
