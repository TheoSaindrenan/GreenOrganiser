1. Purpose

These General Terms and Conditions of Sale (hereinafter “GTC”) govern the contractual relationship between GREEN ORGANISER (hereinafter “the Company”) and partner hotels (hereinafter “the Partner”) in the context of marketing eco-friendly accommodation for groups.

2. Services provided

The Company offers an intermediary service consisting of:

  • Receiving requests for quotes from Customers wishing to book eco-friendly accommodation for a group (minimum 10 rooms or equivalent),
  • Forwarding these requests to the Partner,
  • Negotiating and presenting an offer to the Customer,
  • Facilitating contact and the conclusion of the booking.

The Company does not act as an accommodation provider and does not directly provide accommodation services.

3. Commission

In return for its intermediary service, the Company receives a commission of 10% of the total amount excluding VAT of the booking confirmed by the Customer. The commission is payable by the Partner upon firm confirmation of the booking by the Customer. The commission is calculated on the total amount of the nights and services booked by the Customer through the Company.

Any booking cancelled by the Customer does not give rise to the payment of commission, unless cancellation fees are charged by the Partner, in which case the commission also applies to these fees.

4. Invoicing and payment terms

An invoice corresponding to the amount of the commission is issued by the Company to the Partner upon confirmation of the booking. Invoices are payable within 30 days of their date of issue, by bank transfer.

Any delay in payment will result, after formal notice, in the application of late payment interest at the legal rate in force, as well as a fixed compensation of €40 for recovery costs (Article L441-10 of the Commercial Code).
